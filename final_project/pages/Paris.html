• Paris is the City of Love

    • There is a reason that people repeat the Audrey Hepburn “Paris is always a good idea.” line from the movie Sabrina! Paris is one city that is on a lot of people’s wish list. The annual number of visitors keeping Paris in the top 3 destinations confirms that. Paris is known by several monikers/nicknames one of which is the City Of Love. This beautiful, picturesque city is most often associated with romance. Flocks of newlyweds and starry-eyed couples in love head to Paris to celebrate their togetherness. Many romantic movies are filmed here and many authors base their love stories in Paris.
